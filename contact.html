Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access **Wholesale (B2B) pricing** and place bulk orders? ▼

Our **Wholesale Portal** is exclusive to registered business partners. To place bulk orders at wholesale rates, please visit the Wholesale Portal. New partners must complete a brief registration and verification process for account access.

What is the average shipping time for large items like AC units? ▼

Shipping for **Air Conditioning units** typically takes **3-5 business days** depending on regional logistics. Smaller items usually arrive within 1-3 days. You will receive a tracking number within 24 hours of purchase to monitor progress.

What components are covered by the **2-Year Limited Warranty**? ▼

The warranty covers **core mechanical and electrical components** against manufacturer defects, including the compressor and main circuit board. It does not cover damage from incorrect installation, power surges, or standard consumables (e.g., filters, refrigerants). Full terms are detailed here.

Do you accept **Mobile Money** for individual purchases? ▼

Yes, we offer multiple payment options, including **Mobile Money** (MTN, Vodafone, etc.), major credit cards (Visa/Mastercard), and secure bank transfer for B2B accounts. All payment gateways meet current security standards.

Can Genix Cool provide AC unit **installation services**? ▼

We focus on sales and supply, but we maintain a network of **certified, independent installation partners** who are familiar with our units. We highly recommend using a certified technician to ensure your warranty remains valid and the unit performs optimally.
